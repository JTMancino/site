The Nothing Paradox

The origin of the world is really interesting. Lots of arguments from theology to axiarxhism try to explain where "stuff" comes from, as the ancients said, nihilo nihil fit. Another paper I'm working on is an exploration of this, and in particular, the question of how and why something comes from nothing. To keep it very brief, I try to solve this by redefining "nothing" as the metaphysical priority state or the state which is characterized by being efficiently uncaused.

Historic and Platonic Socrates

I am working on a history of philosophy paper about the transition in Plato's dialogues between the early and middle corpus. Most scholars seem to agree on "Meno" as the critical point of the transition, but I argue there's enough evidence to consider "Phaedo" as a reasonable alternative. I might update this website with a papers page to post my writing in lieu of real publication.
